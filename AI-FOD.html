Strategic Data Organization

To ensure safety-critical reliability, data is segregated into three distinct tiers.

🏋️

1. Primary T&V

**Training & Validation.** The core dataset used to teach the model.

  • High-res runway images (1024x1024)
  • Classes: Bolts, tools, birds, plastic
  • Backgrounds: Tire marks, seams, oil
  • Split: 80% Train / 20% Val
🛡️

2. Edge Case (A-Set)

**Adversarial & Hard Cases.** Used to fine-tune and harden the model.

  • Occlusion: Partially hidden debris
  • Low Contrast: Black rubber on asphalt
  • Motion Blur: Windy conditions
  • Extreme scale variations
🎬

3. Simulation (RWS)

**Real-World Simulation.** Strictly for final deployment testing.

  • Calculates False Positive Rate (FPR)
  • Calculates False Negative Rate (FNR)
  • Controlled FOD drops
  • Mimics exact live stream latency
